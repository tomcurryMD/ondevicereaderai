## Full Description (4000 chars max)

OnDeviceReaderAI is a free document reader that reads your books aloud with natural-sounding AI voices — entirely on your device, no subscription required.

🎧 AI VOICES THAT SOUND NATURAL
Unlike robotic system TTS, OnDeviceReaderAI uses Piper neural text-to-speech powered by sherpa-onnx. Choose from multiple voice models that speak with natural intonation, rhythm, and pacing. Download once, use forever — no internet needed after setup.

📖 READ ANYTHING
Open ePub books, PDF documents, and HTML files directly from your device. Import from your file manager, or open files from other apps. OnDeviceReaderAI renders your content beautifully with customizable themes, fonts, and spacing.

🔒 PRIVATE & OFFLINE
Your books never leave your device. All AI processing happens locally — no cloud servers, no data collection, no accounts. Once you download a voice model, the entire app works without internet.

✨ POLISHED READING EXPERIENCE
• Three themes: Light, Dark, and Sepia
• Adjustable font size, line spacing, and font family
• Table of Contents navigation
• Bookmarks with position tracking
• Reading progress saved automatically
• Resume exactly where you left off

🎵 SMART READ-ALOUD
• Sentence-by-sentence highlighting as text is spoken
• Auto-scroll follows the narration
• Play, pause, resume, and stop controls
• Adjustable speech speed (0.5x to 2.0x)
• Auto-advances through chapters
• Background playback with notification controls
• Bluetooth and headset support via MediaSession

📚 LIBRARY MANAGEMENT
• Grid and list view modes
• Sort by title, author, recently read, or recently added
• Search across your library
• Cover art extraction and display

🗣️ AVAILABLE VOICES
• Amy (US English) — Medium quality, 63 MB
• Lessac (US English) — Medium quality, 63 MB
• LibriTTS (US English) — Medium quality, 75 MB
• Alba (UK English) — Medium quality, 63 MB
• Amy Low (US English) — Lighter model, 16 MB

WHY OnDeviceReaderAI?

Most read-aloud apps either sound robotic (system TTS) or charge $100+/year for natural voices (Speechify, NaturalReader). OnDeviceReaderAI gives you natural AI voices for free, running entirely on your phone. No subscription, no cloud dependency, no data harvesting.

Perfect for:
• Commuters who want to listen to ePubs
• Students studying from PDF textbooks
• Readers with visual impairments or reading difficulties
• Anyone who prefers listening to reading
• People who value privacy and offline functionality

OPEN SOURCE TECHNOLOGY
Built with Piper TTS (neural text-to-speech), sherpa-onnx (on-device AI runtime), and Jetpack Compose (modern Android UI). Voice models are sourced from the open-source Piper voices project.
